Detroit Became Human

Detroit: Become Human is a narrative-driven video game set in a futuristic world where androids, created to serve humans, begin to develop consciousness and emotions. The game, developed by Quantic Dream, follows three main characters: Kara, a housekeeper android; Connor, a prototype android sent to hunt down deviant androids; and Markus, who leads a revolution for android rights.
